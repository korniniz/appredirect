Desktop App Launchers

When you click a link below, your browser may show a popup such as:
“This site is trying to open Outlook/Teams/Word/Excel/PowerPoint”.
Please click Allow / Open to launch the desktop app.
If the desktop app isn’t available, use the Open on the web option on each page.

Outlook – Mail

Launch Outlook desktop (Mail). Fallback to Outlook Web.

Outlook – Calendar

Launch Outlook desktop (Calendar). Fallback to Outlook Web Month view.

Microsoft Teams

Launch Teams desktop. Fallback to Teams Web.

Microsoft Word

Launch Word desktop. Fallback to Word Online.

Microsoft Excel

Launch Excel desktop. Fallback to Excel Online.

Microsoft PowerPoint

Launch PowerPoint desktop. Fallback to PowerPoint Online.
